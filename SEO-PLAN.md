## Important Compliance Direction

Avoid these claims:

- guaranteed profit
- stable monthly income
- no loss
- safe investment
- passive income guaranteed
- account flipping

Use safer wording:

- risk planning
- conservative setup
- demo testing
- educational tool
- risk-controlled settings
- not financial advice
