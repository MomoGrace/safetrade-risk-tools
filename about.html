About BytesTrade

BytesTrade is a free forex risk tools website built to help traders understand position size, drawdown, risk reward, and prop firm account limits.

Our purpose

Many traders focus on entry signals but ignore risk. BytesTrade focuses on the practical numbers that should be checked before a trade is placed: how much is at risk, how large the position is, how much drawdown is possible, and whether the potential reward is reasonable.

What we provide

  • Free forex lot size calculator
  • Free drawdown calculator
  • Free risk reward calculator
  • Educational guides about forex risk management and prop firm rules
